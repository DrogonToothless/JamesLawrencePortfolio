Teen Program

The main goal of Utah Valley Taekwondo is to take our students and build up their self-esteem. We do this by understanding their goals and accomplishments and recognizing and rewarding them for it, and their efforts too.

Developed Traits

  • ✔ Self-esteem
  • ✔ Focus
  • ✔ Self-discipline
  • ✔ Physical fitness
  • ✔ Respect
  • ✔ Patience
  • ✔ Avoiding conflicts
  • ✔ Dealing with bullies
  • ✔ Stranger awareness
  • ✔ Saying no to peer pressure

Adult Program

Our adult students typically discover that after being involved with our program, they manage stress better, sleep better at night, are able to progress better at work, and feel better overall. If they are ever faced with a dangerous situation, they feel better knowing that they can defend themselves and neutralize the situation.

Developed Traits

  • ✔ Increased cardio vascular fitness
  • ✔ Improved muscle tone
  • ✔ Increased energy
  • ✔ Greater flexibility
  • ✔ Lower stress levels
  • ✔ Higher self esteem
  • ✔ Respect
  • ✔ Patience
  • ✔ Emotional satisfaction
  • ✔ Better concentration and focus
