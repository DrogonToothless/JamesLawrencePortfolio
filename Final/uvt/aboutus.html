About Utah Valley Taekwondo

Utah Valley Taekwondo was founded in 2024 by Master Geoffrey Payne. Master Payne has always had a passion for both teaching and Taekwondo. And when he put them together, he created one of the biggest and most highly-rated martial arts schools in Utah.
